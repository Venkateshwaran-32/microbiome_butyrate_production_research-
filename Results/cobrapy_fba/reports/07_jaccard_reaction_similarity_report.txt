07_jaccard_reaction_pairs_long.csv
Path: Results/cobrapy_fba/tables/07_jaccard_reaction_pairs_long.csv
Row grain: one row per ordered species pair (100 rows for 10 species)
Columns:
- species_a: First species in the ordered pair; SBML file stem.
- species_b: Second species in the ordered pair; SBML file stem.
- n_reactions_a: Size of species_a's reaction-ID set.
- n_reactions_b: Size of species_b's reaction-ID set.
- intersection: Number of reaction IDs shared between species_a and species_b.
- union: Number of distinct reaction IDs across the two species combined.
- jaccard: Jaccard similarity between the two reaction sets. Formula: jaccard = intersection / union
- dissimilarity: Jaccard dissimilarity; complementary distance metric used for hierarchical
  clustering. Formula: dissimilarity = 1 - jaccard
