04_micom_community_growth_summary_by_diet.csv
Path: Results/micom_fba/tables/04_micom_community_growth_summary_by_diet.csv
Row grain: one row per diet_name
Columns:
- diet_name: Diet scenario name from Medium_files/diet.csv.
- solver_status: MICOM solver status returned for the cooperative tradeoff solve.
- community_growth_rate: MICOM-reported community growth rate for the solved diet.
- objective_value: Objective value returned by MICOM for this cooperative tradeoff solve. Formula:
  objective_value = optimization objective reported by MICOM after cooperative_tradeoff
- tradeoff_fraction: Tradeoff fraction passed into MICOM cooperative tradeoff.
- num_taxa_total: Number of taxa returned in the MICOM members table for this diet.
- num_taxa_with_nonzero_growth: Count of taxa whose growth rate exceeded the growth threshold.
  Formula: num_taxa_with_nonzero_growth = count(growth_rate > 1e-09)
- matched_diet_metabolites: Count of diet metabolite IDs translated into MICOM medium entries.
  Formula: matched_diet_metabolites = number of diet metabolite_ids with a MICOM medium mapping
- missing_diet_metabolites: Count of diet metabolite IDs that could not be translated into MICOM
  medium entries. Formula: missing_diet_metabolites = total diet metabolite_ids -
  matched_diet_metabolites

04_micom_taxon_growth_by_diet.csv
Path: Results/micom_fba/tables/04_micom_taxon_growth_by_diet.csv
Row grain: one row per diet_name x taxon_id
Columns:
- diet_name: Diet scenario name from Medium_files/diet.csv.
- taxon_id: MICOM taxon/model identifier.
- abundance: Taxon abundance used in the equal-abundance baseline taxonomy table. Formula: abundance
  = 1 / number of taxa in the baseline community
- growth_rate: MICOM-reported taxon growth rate for this diet.
- is_growing: Boolean flag showing whether the MICOM taxon growth rate exceeded the growth
  threshold. Formula: is_growing = growth_rate > 1e-09
- reactions: Number of reactions present in the MICOM member model returned for this taxon.
- metabolites: Number of metabolites present in the MICOM member model returned for this taxon.
