CSV Output Dictionary

06c_sg90_subject_abnormality_review.csv
Path: Results/subject_level_fba/tables/06c_sg90_subject_abnormality_review.csv
Row grain: one row per flagged SG90 subject
Columns:
- subject_id: Subject identifier from the SG90 subject-level MICOM run.
- age_group: Age-bin label assigned from the subject metadata.
- diets_affected: Comma-separated list of diets whose outputs contributed to the abnormality flag.
- flag_category: Semicolon-separated list of abnormality categories assigned to this subject.
- evidence_values: Compact evidence string summarizing the key numeric values that triggered the
  flags.
- driver_pattern: Diet-specific summary labels describing whether the subject showed sparse, broad,
  or dominant taxon-growth structure.
- short_interpretation: Plain-English interpretation combining the flagged evidence and driver
  summaries.
- overall_assessment: Top-level review classification for the flagged subject. Formula:
  overall_assessment is assigned from the flag combination: technical issue takes precedence, then
  sparse-but-valid case, then biologically interesting outlier
