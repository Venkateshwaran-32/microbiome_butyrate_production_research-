PCA design
- Rows are subjects.
- Columns are reaction_id values.
- Values are signed flux values.
- Taxon-level rows are collapsed by summing flux within subject_id + reaction_id.
- Medium exchange rows are excluded with is_medium == FALSE.
- Missing subject-reaction pairs are filled with 0.
- Flux values are transformed with asinh before PCA.
- PCA uses prcomp with centering and scaling.
